Explore the Magical World of Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a world-renowned animation studio known for its stunning visuals, imaginative storytelling, and emotional depth.

Founded in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki, Isao Takahata, and Toshio Suzuki. Known for its visually stunning hand-drawn animation and rich narratives, Ghibli has produced some of the most beloved and critically acclaimed animated films, including Princess Mononoke (1997), Spirited Away (2001), My Neighbor Totoro (1988), Ponyo (2008) and Howl's Moving Castle (2004). Their films often explore themes of nature, friendship, and courage.
